# Privacy Policy - Okan Chrome Extension

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## Data Collection

Okan does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

## How It Works

- All communication happens between your local terminal and your local browser via `localhost`
- No data is sent to any external server
- No analytics, no tracking, no cookies
- No user accounts required

## Permissions

Okan requests the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| `tabs` | To send notifications to browser tabs |
| `storage` | To save your preferences (mode, language, badge position) locally |
| `scripting` | To inject the notification badge into web pages |
| `alarms` | To maintain a reliable connection to the local server |

All data stays on your machine.
